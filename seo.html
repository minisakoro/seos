05 - Action Plan

What Needs Fixing

#1
Critical

No homepage meta description or title keywords

Title tag is just the brand name — no keywords. Meta description is completely missing. Google auto-generates snippets, meaning search engines have zero context about what you sell.

#2
Critical

Product descriptions critically thin (60-100 words)

Product pages average 60-100 words vs. 300+ minimum. For an e-commerce store where customers can't touch products, detailed descriptions covering materials and specs are essential for both SEO and conversions.

#3
Critical

GTM loaded 13 times on homepage

Each section builder injects its own GTM script copy. Massive wasted bandwidth and CPU on every pageview. Should load exactly once in the head.

#4
Critical

763 KB HTML with 123 script tags

Homepage HTML is 7.6x the target size. Third-party apps inject massive inline JSON config. Combined with 123 scripts, this severely impacts LCP and INP metrics.

#5
Critical

Care Guide page returns 404

Linked in main navigation and footer. A broken expertise page on a brand's site signals neglect to both users and search engines. This should be a comprehensive guide.

#6
Critical

~60 junk collections indexed (out of 143)

Internal layout fragments, test pages, and expired promos are all in the sitemap. Nearly half of all collections are diluting crawl budget and creating thin content signals.

#7
Critical

Zero product reviews on-site

Missing aggregateRating in schema means no star ratings in Google SERPs. Static homepage testimonials don't compensate for missing product-level social proof.

#8
Critical

Schema markup has multiple errors

@context uses http:// instead of https://. Organization sameAs contains empty strings. Invalid schema prevents rich snippets from appearing in search results.

#9
High

FAQ contradicts homepage messaging

FAQ says products include "alloy metal mix costume jewelry" while homepage says "all of our jewelry is made with REAL materials." This directly undermines brand trust and negatively impacts product quality signals for search engines.

#10
High

12 utility pages indexed that shouldn't be

Email capture pages, waitlist pages, thank-you pages, and expired campaign pages are all in the sitemap. Users can land on these from Google with zero context, which wastes crawl budget and creates a poor user experience.
